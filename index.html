An online community for scientific researchers, reserach managers and industry specialists to discuss policies, needs and opportunities in the field of application of science and technology in different sectors in Iraq. Project reviews and strategy debate are encouraged to support the role of science and technology in the socio-economic development of Iraq.

Participation is sought of individuals, NGO's and government agencies within Iraq, Diaspora and all interested international individuals and organizations.

FAQ

Is the platform restricted to specific science and technology disciplines?
No. All disciplines of science and engineering are supported by this platform.
Is membership open to non Iraqi individuals and organizations?
Sure. Membership is open for all interested individuals and organizations from all over the world.
